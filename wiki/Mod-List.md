# Mod List

Categorised overview of every mod in **Brassroots**. The pack contains roughly **370 mods** on **Minecraft 1.21.1 / NeoForge**. This list is grouped by purpose, not alphabetically — use Ctrl+F to find a specific mod.

> Mod count and exact composition can change between releases. The authoritative list at any moment is the contents of the pack's `mods/` directory.
